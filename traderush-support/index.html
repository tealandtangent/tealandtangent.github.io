TradeRush – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

TradeRush: Daily Trading Game ("the App") is developed and operated by Teal & Tangent Ventures LLC. This policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

Information We Collect

TradeRush uses Apple Game Center to support leaderboard functionality. Through Game Center, the App may access your Game Center alias and leaderboard scores. This data is managed by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy (apple.com/privacy).

TradeRush displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain device information — including your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) — to serve personalized advertisements. This data is collected and managed by Google and governed by Google's Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy). On iOS 14 and later, the App will request your permission before accessing your advertising identifier via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You may opt out at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Data Sharing

Game Center data is used solely to display leaderboard rankings within the App. We do not use this data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

Advertising data collected through AdMob is shared with Google for the purpose of serving third-party advertisements within the App. We do not sell or share your data with any other third parties. Apple's and Google's handling of data collected through their respective platforms is subject to their own privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

TradeRush offers optional in-app purchases (monthly archive vault unlocks) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Purchase records are managed locally on your device. Apple's handling of payment data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is managed by Apple Game Center. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.
