TradeRush – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

TradeRush: Daily Trading Game ("the App") is developed and operated by Teal & Tangent Ventures LLC. This policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

Information We Collect

TradeRush uses Apple Game Center to support leaderboard functionality. Through Game Center, the App may access your Game Center alias and leaderboard scores. This data is managed by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy (apple.com/privacy).

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information on our own servers.

Data Sharing

Game Center data is used solely to display leaderboard rankings within the App. We do not use this data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Apple's handling of Game Center data is subject to their own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is managed by Apple Game Center.
