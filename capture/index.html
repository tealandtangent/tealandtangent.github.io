The problem

Your guests are walking off your property to spend money somewhere else

On-property restaurants and services lose guests to outside options every day. Not because guests dislike them. Because no one made the case at the right moment.

A guest assumes your steakhouse is too expensive and heads off-property for dinner. They never looked at the menu. A targeted offer at the right moment could have changed that decision and kept their spend on your property for the rest of the night.

Push notifications reach your whole guest list, including guests who already have plans, guests across the property, and guests who checked out yesterday. The message gets ignored. Over time, so do all your notifications.

Solutions that do target by proximity require guests to download a separate app. They will not. You are back to square one.

The moment a guest is nearby and still deciding is the only moment that matters. Capture owns that moment.

How it works

Three steps to capturing the spend that is already on your property

Capture embeds in the app your guests already have. You control who participates and set the guardrails. Your on-property businesses handle the rest.

01

Embed into your existing venue app

Capture is a drop-in SDK. One integration, and every opted-in guest is instantly reachable by proximity. No new app for guests to download. No new product for your team to maintain.

02

You decide which businesses participate

You decide which on-property businesses can send offers and define the rules. Each business manages their own offers within the boundaries you set. You stay in control of the guest experience at all times.

03

The right guest gets the right offer at the right moment

When a guest is physically near a participating business, they receive a relevant offer at the exact moment impulse works in your favor. Not a blast to your whole list. A nudge to the right guest at the right moment.
