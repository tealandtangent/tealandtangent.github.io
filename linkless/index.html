FAQ

Common questions

Does this require guests to download a new app?

No. Linkless embeds directly into your existing venue app. Guests who already have your app are instantly eligible.

What if our venue has poor indoor signal?

That's exactly where Linkless works best. It runs on Bluetooth, not GPS or cell signal, so it performs reliably in dense indoor environments like casino floors and resort lobbies.

Can we control which businesses send offers and how often?

Yes. Each business manages its own offers and proximity radius through a dashboard. You control the rules at the venue level.

Do guests have to opt in?

Yes. Offers only reach guests who have opted in through your existing app. No cold messaging.

What venues is this built for?

Casinos, resorts, stadiums, and malls. Any venue with an existing app, opted-in guests, and multiple on-property businesses competing for foot traffic.

How long does integration take?

We're currently working with early partners to establish a timeline. Reach out and we'll give you a realistic estimate for your stack.

Why not build this ourselves?

Bluetooth proximity infrastructure is complex to build, calibrate, and maintain at scale. Linkless is a drop-in SDK with a management dashboard. Your team stays focused on the guest experience. We own the proximity layer.
