ConTXT Card – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

ConTXT Card is designed to be simple, private, and secure. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data outside of your device.

Information We Collect

ConTXT Card does not collect any personal information. All data you enter (name, phone, email, one-liners, notes) is stored only on your device, using Apple's local storage.

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Send your data to any servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your usage
  • Use analytics or advertising

Your information never leaves your device unless you choose to share it via another app.

Data Storage

All content remains on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

ConTXT Card does not collect any data and may be safely used by minors.
