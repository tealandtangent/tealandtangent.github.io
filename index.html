About

Teal & Tangent is a family holding company led by Nirav Suraiya. We operate with a simple mandate: build resilient income, protect downside, and explore new ideas. We prefer small, repeatable advantages over big, fragile bets. We measure success in cash flow, financial freedom, and quality of life.

Operating Principles:

  • Write It Down: rules beat vibes
  • Family Aligned: all decisions must support the life we truly want
